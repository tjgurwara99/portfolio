Enter into the mysterious secret garden and embrace the joys of mouth-wateringly delicious food and exquisite cocktails inspired by the lavish gardens around you. Let the magic of our extraordinary offerings whisk you away on a culinary adventure to another world you can only dream of. For those who plan to party until dawn, a delicious breakfast will be the end of an absolutely unforgettable night in your very own secret gardens. You shall not be thirsty in the Secret Garden! Expect deliciously exotic cocktails crafted especially for you by our fantastic mixologists. There will be something for everyone and if alcohol is not your thing, just tell our cocktail staff and they'll make sure you can taste our Secret Garden elixirs. For those who enjoy the spirits, a shot or two and beer, we have two bars to make sure you have what you need! Of course, water in the Secret Garden will be available everywhere and in the early hours of the morning, you can count on us to provide tea, coffee and hot chocolate to sober you up. There will be plenty of vegan, gluten-free and nut-free options as we believe that everyone should be able to indulge, no matter your dietary preferences!
